Let's Begin!

The purpose of this assessment is to provide directional clarity around issues that may already feel familiar. Most fulfillment problems don't appear as obvious failures; they surface gradually through margin erosion, chargebacks, strained buyer relationships, and ongoing operational friction.

This assessment applies to brands selling direct-to-consumer, wholesale, or through national retail partners. Several of the risks below tend to intensify once retail compliance, routing guides, EDI requirements, and chargebacks enter the picture.

This assessment is intentionally brief. Its goal is not to diagnose every issue, but to help surface where to focus attention and which questions are worth asking next.
